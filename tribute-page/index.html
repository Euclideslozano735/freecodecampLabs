Nelson Mandela

El hombre que vio hacerse realidad muchos de sus sueños.

Here's a time line of Nelson Mandela's life:


  • July 18, 1918: Born in Mvezo, South Africa.
  • 1943: Joins the African National Congress (ANC)
  • 1944: Co-founds the ANC Youth League.
  • 1952: Opens South Africa's first black law firm with Oliver Tambo.
  • 1961: Becomes commander-in-chief of Umkhonto we Sizwe, the armed wing of the ANC.
  • 1964–1990: Imprisoned for sabotage, spending 18 years on Robben Island.
  • February 11, 1990: Released from prison.
  • 1993: Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize.
  • April 27, 1994: Votes in South Africa's first democratic election.
  • May 10, 1994: Inaugurated as President of South Africa.
  • December 5, 2013: Passes away at age 95.
